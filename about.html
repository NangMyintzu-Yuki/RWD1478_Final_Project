For The Music

PDT was formed over 20 years ago by a small but dedicated group of musical instrument enthusiasts brought together by a love of music and a shared vision.
The world may have changed a lot over last twenty years and PDT is no exception. The team remains specializing in the production of musical instruments over various genres across the globe.

Martin Smith

Based in the small village of St.Neots just outside Cambridge the Martin Smith team have been developing, manufacturing and perfecting a range of guitars, ukulele’s and tuners for the last 12 years. Through continual iterative improvement this offering now represents some of the best selling guitars on Amazon platform in the UK and is largely known as a provider of quality entry level products.
